## 📋 About This App

ssa-my-social-security-browser-automation is a tool that helps you do certain tasks on the Social Security Administration (SSA) website automatically. You can use it to get benefit verification letters, check your earnings record, download SSA-1099 tax forms, review benefit estimates, and manage your account.

This app works on Windows and runs in your web browser without needing special technical skills. It automates repetitive steps, so you do not have to click through the site manually every time.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

- Automatically log in to the SSA my Social Security portal.
- Download and save benefit verification letters.
- View and save your earnings record.
- Retrieve your SSA-1099 tax form quickly.
- Check benefit estimates based on your account info.
- Handle common account tasks faster and easier.
- No programming needed to use this app.

## 🔑 How to Use the App

1. **Log in**  
   Enter your my Social Security username and password on the login screen.  

2. **Select Task**  
   Choose which task you want to automate, for example:  
   - Download benefit verification letter  
   - View earnings record  
   - Get SSA-1099 tax form  
   - Check benefit estimates  
   - Manage account info  

3. **Start Automation**  
   Click the **Start** button. The app will open a browser window and log into SSA for you.  

4. **Save Files**  
   When documents or reports download, select where to save them on your computer. The app will notify you when the task finishes.

5. **Close the App**  
   After your work is done, close the app window.
